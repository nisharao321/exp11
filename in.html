GEORESISTIVITY TEST

Objective

To obtain the resistivity of the ground using Schlumberger Array with the help of Georesistivity meter.


Apparatus used:

Georesistivity meter, Clips and wire, Battery, Electrodes and Hammer.

STEP  1 

connect the current electrode to the positive side of the resistivity meter.

STEP  2 

connect the current electrode to the negative side of the resistivity meter.

STEP  3 

connect the potential electrode to the positive side of the resistivity meter.

STEP  4 

connect the current electrode to the negative side of the resistivity meter.

STEP  5 

connect the resistivity meter to the battery.
